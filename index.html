About me

Wellcome to my Portfolio, I'm Elias a Game Programmer & Designer.

My main interests are gameplay and AI. I love making alive all kinds of game mechanics and tweaking them until they feel awesome, such as jump feeling, shooting feedback, etc. For me, it's like taking ideas or conceptions and making them real and playable for the others, making them feel fast, agile, strong, fear depending on the mechanics objectives. " I'm a mad scientist, It's so cool! "

Actually, I'm cursing at mid time a Master Degree of Game Programming and Design at the University of IIM in Paris, France. In my free time I work on some personal projects and of course learning new thinks every time i can.

Here you can view my résumé, a few of my personal projects,such a game jams or school projects and some fun coding or game stuff I do in my free time.

I’d love to chat about job opportunities, projects I’ve worked on, diversity in tech, memes, or whatever is on your mind.
